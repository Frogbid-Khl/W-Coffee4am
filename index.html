1. What is an NFT?

NFT stands for “Non Fungible Token” and it refers to the first ever type of “digital property” we humans can own. Once you purchase an NFT the act of purchasing it gets engraved into a Blockchain and will forever be there assigning ownership of that digital asset to you, that is until you sell it.

2. How do I buy a 4AM Coffee Punk?

4AM Coffee Punks can be bought here on our website, unless there is a SOLD out sign. In that case you will have to buy it in the secondary market at OpenSea.
You have two ways you can buy an NFT with us at Café 4AM:
Self-Service:

1. Convert your local money (MXN or USD usually) to a popular cryptocurrency such as Bitcoin, Ethereum or USDT.
2. Once converted, convert that cryptocurrency into $SOL, the official currency of the Solana blockchain where 4AM Coffee Punks live.
3. Once you have Solana, download the Phantom App and deposit your funds to it.
4. With your Phantom app logged in, click the “MINT NOW” button on this website and that’s it - You are ready to go!

Sounds too complicated? Let us help you.

Manual Service:

1. Download the phantom app and get your solana address like you see here.

3. Café 4AM will charge an extra 10% as a processing fee.
